Summary: Lattitude vs X

The point of this project is to gather data and create charts to visualize the affect (if any) the latitude has on various weather elements (Temp, Wind, Humidity, & Cloud Coverage).

This website was to make the results readily available in a clear and simple way. Please make your way around the site by navigating the nav bar or by clicking on the various plots
